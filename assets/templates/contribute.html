How To Contribute

This site is only made possible by the contribution that you make.

Please take a few seconds after you received that "Your Alexa skill ... is now live!" ✌️ email to post your certification time.

Twitter

Super simple! Post a message with the #skillcertificationtime hash tag and the number of days.

You can also include the skill name and a link to it. The sky is the limit, unless your message not exceed the 280 characters length! See this example.

If you prefer to report it privately, just send a DM to @skillcerttimes and it will be accepted as well.

Alexa Skill

It's not ready yet but soon you'll be able to submit your certification times from an Alexa enabled device!

As soon as the Alexa Skill is available, more details will be shared.
